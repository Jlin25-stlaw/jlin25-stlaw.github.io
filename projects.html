About Me

My name is Jlin Brown. I am a student-athlete at St. Lawrence University who is interested in design, technology, and leadership. I enjoy building simple, clear websites that feel easy to use and look good on any device.

In class, I’ve worked on projects like color palettes for a subscription gardening service, registration forms for a racing event, and media projects about banned books. On the court, I’ve learned how communication, discipline, and preparation carry over into every part of my life.
